Leaflet Map Displaying OpenFlights.org Data

Saru Mehta

April 11 2016

Used Clustering Markers Plugin to show information about the 6,977 airports across the world.

Clustering Levels are indicated by Yellow, Green, and Blue circles/balloons.

The Yellow circles on the map represent areas where the user can click to zoom in to see more clustering groups.

Clicking on the Green circles show that they contain more green clustering groups or standalone sites(blue balloon).

    Field IATA/FAA
  • 3-letter FAA code, for airports located in the USA.
  • 3-letter IATA code, for all other airports.
